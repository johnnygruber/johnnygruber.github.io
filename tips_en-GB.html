Below we have compiled a few tips about interval training for you.

Motivation

In daily training many runners make the mistake to always run at the same pace.

In the long term this results in a stop of power and no real increase in fitness.

One of the most effective methods to achieve performance improvement is the interval training.

The interval training is a switch of fast running phases with slow trot or walk breaks.

So a loading and unloading in a balanced mix.

By the load peaks the body is brought beyond its borders and adapts to the new strain.

You should first start with the interval training if you are able to run at least half an hour at a time without any problems.

Anyone who is unsure should definitely consult a doctor to check his fitness before he starts the training. If there are no medical objections, you can start.

If you are already running three times a week, you can replace one workout with an interval training.

Intervall training pases

Interval training consists of three phases:

  • Warm up
  • High speed phase followed by a low speed recovery period - several repetitions
  • Cool down

Warm up

Start the interval training always with a warm up unit in the endurance pace .

Ideal for example, are two kilometers in the quiet pace.

Your muscles heated and the cardiovascular system gets used to it slow to the increasing demand.

Length of high speed units

You can do interval training based on a certain time or a given distance.

The IntervallLogger app uses the distance model. You define the respective path lengths of the various units for your training individual.

The tempo units can extend from 50 meters to 3000 meters.

Units of 50 meters to 1000 promote the development of the speed (anaerobic training).

Longer loads from 1000 meters are primarily the endurance training (aerobic training).

A good load distance is eg. 400 meters.

This distance is especially suitable for beginners in interval training.

Not overdo it with the pace. Remember, this rate should also be able to retain in the last iteration.

Length of slow units

A good and simple method is the same length as line to vote at the pace unit (Principle load = discharge ).

The pace in the recovery period can or should be perfectly very slow be, for example, Traben slow or fast walking.

But stay on all cases in motion , as can be degraded more quickly in the muscle lactate accumulated by the movement.

Number of repetitions

A good home in the interval training include five replicates of 400 meters and load 400 meters recovery period.

If you ( 200 e.g. meters) choose a shorter load units, you can certainly even more reps plan (eg, ten ).

Cool down

Complete the interval training always with a unit in the endurance pace .

Are ideal, as well as the warm-up, two kilometers in the quiet pace.
